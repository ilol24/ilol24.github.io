About the Movie

"The Devil Wears Prada" is a 2006 film directed by David Frankel and based on Lauren Weisberger's 2003 novel of the same name. The story follows Andy Sachs, a young woman in New York who lands a job as a personal assistant to the editor-in-chief of Runway magazine, Miranda Priestly. Throughout the movie, Andy discovers where she fits in life and who are the people she wants to surround herself with.

Cast

  • Anne Hathaway as Andy Sachs
  • Meryl Streep as Miranda Priestly
  • Emily Blunt as Emily Charlton
  • Stanley Tucci as Nigel
